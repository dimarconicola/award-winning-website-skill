## Color Mode Guidance

| Mode | When | Palette Approach |
|------|------|------------------|
| Dark | cinematic, luxury, futuristic | Deep bg (#0a-#12), light fg, accent pops |
| Light | editorial, minimal, artisanal | Off-white bg (#f5-#fff), dark fg, muted accent |
