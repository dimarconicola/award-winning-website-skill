# Design Vocabulary: Vibes

A shared language for aesthetic direction. Pick ONE primary, ONE secondary.

---

## Primary Vibes

### `minimal`
Maximum whitespace, restrained palette, surgical precision.

**Signals:** Confidence, clarity, nothing to hide
**Typography:** Geometric sans, clean weights
**Color:** Neutral dominant, single accent used sparingly
**Motion:** Subtle or none. If present, ultra-refined.
**When:** SaaS, tools, products that "just work"

### `cinematic`
Dramatic scale, hero video/imagery, theatrical reveals.

**Signals:** Story, emotion, spectacle
**Typography:** Condensed display, dramatic scale jumps
**Color:** Dark dominant, high contrast, moody
**Motion:** Scroll-driven reveals, slow entrances
**When:** Entertainment, launches, immersive experiences

### `editorial`
Magazine-inspired, strong typography hierarchy, content-first.

**Signals:** Authority, craft, long-form worthiness
**Typography:** Serif display + sans body, strong size contrast
**Color:** Paper-like backgrounds, ink-like text
**Motion:** Minimal, text-focused reveals
**When:** Publications, agencies, thought leadership

### `playful`
Bright colors, bouncy motion, whimsical illustrations.

**Signals:** Fun, approachable, memorable
**Typography:** Rounded or quirky sans, variable weights
**Color:** Saturated, multi-color, gradients OK
**Motion:** Bouncy eases, stagger, scale pops
**When:** Consumer apps, youth brands, creative tools

### `brutal`
Raw, exposed structure, monospace type, anti-design.

**Signals:** Authenticity, no bullshit, developer aesthetic
**Typography:** Monospace exclusively, strict grid
**Color:** Black/white dominant, one highlight max
**Motion:** Instant or none. Intentionally jarring.
**When:** Dev tools, manifestos, art projects

### `luxury`
Rich textures, gold/champagne accents, slow elegance.

**Signals:** Exclusivity, quality, worth waiting for
**Typography:** Elegant serif, thin weights, generous spacing
**Color:** Muted, warm neutrals, metallic accents
**Motion:** Slow, deliberate, never rushed
**When:** Fashion, hospitality, high-end products

### `futuristic`
Gradients, glassmorphism, tech-forward aesthetics.

**Signals:** Innovation, next-gen, cutting edge
**Typography:** Clean sans, sometimes geometric
**Color:** Dark bg, neon accents, gradients
**Motion:** Smooth, continuous, often cursor-led
**When:** Tech products, crypto, AI, developer platforms

### `artisanal`
Hand-crafted feel, organic textures, warm and human.

**Signals:** Care, authenticity, maker culture
**Typography:** Humanist fonts, slightly imperfect
**Color:** Earth tones, warm palette
**Motion:** Organic, breathing, natural eases
**When:** Craft brands, sustainable products, studios

### `experimental`
Boundary-pushing, genre-bending, unexpected combinations.

**Signals:** Creativity, risk, memorability
**Typography:** Unconventional, rule-breaking
**Color:** Anything goes if intentional
**Motion:** Unusual, generative, cursor-led
**When:** Agencies, artists, portfolios, tech labs

### `bold`
Oversized type, high contrast, unapologetic presence.

**Signals:** Confidence, energy, can't be ignored
**Typography:** Massive display, ultra-black weights
**Color:** High contrast, often B&W + accent
**Motion:** Direct, punchy, no subtlety
**When:** Agencies, statements, launches

### `ethereal`
Soft gradients, floating elements, dreamlike atmosphere.

**Signals:** Calm, transcendence, aspiration
**Typography:** Light weights, generous space
**Color:** Pastels, soft gradients, light dominant
**Motion:** Floating, drifting, gentle
**When:** Wellness, meditation, lifestyle

## Motion Regimes

Pick one primary regime. Note a secondary if combining.

| Regime | Description | When to Use |
|--------|-------------|-------------|
| `subtle` | Micro-interactions only, near-static | Minimal, editorial, content-first |
| `kinetic-type` | Typography as motion protagonist | Bold, editorial, agency |
| `scroll-scene` | Scroll-driven reveals and pins | Product launches, storytelling |
| `immersive` | Full takeover moments | Cinematic, experimental |
| `parallax` | Layered depth, multi-speed | Luxury, cinematic backgrounds |
| `cursor-led` | Cursor drives motion | Experimental, playful, portfolios |
| `minimal-motion` | Nearly static, instant or 100ms fades | Brutal, editorial, performance-first |

## Materiality

The tactile quality of surfaces.

| Label | Characteristics | Best With |
|-------|-----------------|-----------|
| `glass` | Backdrop blur, transparency, frost | Futuristic, minimal |
| `paper` | Subtle shadows, layered cards, tactile | Editorial, artisanal |
| `metal` | Gradients, reflections, brushed surfaces | Luxury, technical |
| `fabric` | Soft edges, organic curves, textile feel | Artisanal, lifestyle |
| `neon` | Glows, saturated, dark backgrounds | Futuristic, playful, gaming |
| `organic` | Natural textures, earth tones, imperfect | Artisanal, sustainable |
